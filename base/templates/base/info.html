Scoring

The scoring system for this league is a fairly standard half PPR format with a boosted DST scoring. I will create a post later contrasting what the DST scores would have looked like had we used it last year, but in the meanwhile you can refer to this post. The bottom line is that DSTs are higher scoring - they start at 0 and are rewarded for good defensive plays, and fractionally penalized per yards and points allowed. They will be higher scoring and hence more important than usual this year.

Waiver Wire

We will be implementing a daily waiver wire system for this league. This means that instead of having waiver claims and decisions go through during a specific period (traditionally Tuesday-Thursday) after which it is a free for all FA pool, we will have this process every non-game day. This is to ensure that waiver wire is not on a first come first serve basis, since mostly everyone in this league works full time.
